Steven Striegel

Steven is a designer based in New York City. He’s currently at Condé Nast designing apps and web experiences for The New Yorker, Vogue, Wired, and others. A committed generalist, his work often spans brand identities, interactive websites, and digital product interfaces. Steven also experiments with analog art forms in video, collage, and photography.
